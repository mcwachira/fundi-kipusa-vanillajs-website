ABOUT US

Fundi Kipusa is a Foundation of young Kenyans and representatives from industry and economy which aims to create jobs in sub-sahara Africa and stop migration. We educate young Kenyans by offering training in various courses.

Our mission is to create attractive future-oriented jobs and build a self-sufficient economy where people can feed themselves.

More than anything, we want people to succeed,
professionally and personally.
Heino Dahmen, founder & Mentor
