ABOUT US

Fundi kipusa is a volunteer based local research, development and training organization that was founded by Heino Dahmen.

We offer training to young Kenyans in fields such as agriculture, engineering and building construction. We are also engaged in cutting edge internet based technologies, key among them being blockchains and cryptocurrencies.

Although Heino Darmen is no longer hands-on in the activities of Fundi Kipusa, he continues to play a vital role as a mentor.

More than anything, we want people to succeed,
professionally and personally.
Heino Dahmen, founder & Mentor
