DeFa-Token

De-Fa stands for Decentralized Farming. We truly believe that the De-Fa movement is a necessary measure for countries to feed themselves and create food security. Our decentralized farming concept contributes to food availability and reduces poverty by generating income for households which are involved in the production, processing and sales process. The Fundi Kipusa Token (FKT) is used to specifically publicize and promote the decentralized farming (DeFa).
