Engineering

The role of our organization is to provide a platform for the youth to learn engineering by providing training on engineering related subjects like fabrication, mechanics and robotics.

What We Do

Research & Development

Through our Engineering center Speed Shop Kenya We carry our research on the latest and modern Engineering technologies.

Education

We also teach the youth on the latest Engineering technologies by practically involving them in building of our I-pots, repairing and modification of cars and building of I.O.T (Internet of things) devices we use at our farm.

Prototyping

Fundi Kipusa is also involved in making ideas come to life through Prototyping. We help individuals and companies who want to bring a product to market in prototyping their products.

Work

CAR BUILDING

Fundi Kipusa as the umbrella company for speed shop kenya is the brain child of Engineer and Mentor Heino Dahmen who has vast experience in the automotive industry.

Our crew works tirelessly to ensure that we meet our target and build vehicles that meet the standards of the 4x4s and Buggy's on a daily basis.

IOT & MECHATRONIC

We are using Iot technologies to solve different problems that We face daily. We are using the Arduino uno at our Hasiera farm to Mo nitor and control the amount of water used in irrigation.We also use a variety of sensors around the green houses in the farm to monitor and collect a wide range of data including temperature and humidity thus enabling us to run the farm efficiently.
