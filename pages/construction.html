Construction

We train the youth on modern construction techniques, as well as how to use computer aided design in developing architectural models. We are also at the forefront in the designing and building of sustainable, environmental friendly houses. We have demonstrated that with the proper knowhow, it is possible to build low cost houses that are visually appealing, occupy only a small ground area (plot) while maximizing on the interior living space.

Design & Modeling

Using Cad software and Blender we design and construct models enabling our clients see architectural models of our proposals. We use this tools to show three-dimensional aspects of a design to our clients.

Education

We teach the youth on the latest Construction technologies.We also offer employment opportunities and enabling them to get Jobs and support themselves.
