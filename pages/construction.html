Construction

The role of our organization is to provide a platform for the youth to learn engineering by providing training on engineering related subjects like fabrication, mechanics and robotics. Therefore we teach young Kenyans modern building technologies and use of computer software to design and develop architectural models.

Design & Modeling

Using Cad software and Blender we design and construct models enabling our clients see architectural models of our proposals. We use this tools to show three-dimensional aspects of a design to our clients.

Education

We teach the youth on the latest Construction technologies.We also offer employment opportunities and enabling them to get Jobs and support themselves.

Construction

At Fundi Kipusa we carry out construction projects. We design and construct buildings, apartment complexes ,tennis courts and other amenities for our clients.
