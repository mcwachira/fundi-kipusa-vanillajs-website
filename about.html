ABOUT US

Fundi Kipusa is a Foundation of young Kenyans and representatives from industry and economy which aims to create jobs in sub-sahara Africa and stop migration.

We educate young Kenyans by offering training in various courses. Our main fields of activity include therefore the modern agriculture, engineering, constructions, mechanics and blockchain.

We follow the principle that all actions should have a positive influence in the global environment from scientific point of view and should create a fair social living space for all people.

Our talented and diverse team works together for one purpose,
to help the youth have a better life.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create attractive future-oriented jobs and build a self-sufficient economy where people can feed themselves.
